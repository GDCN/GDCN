
These are test programs to test preformance of Haskell, E and C.
The preformance is measured by the time it takes for the program to execute.

You may also need to tweak TestE.sh to run your local installation of E.

The algorithms for testing was taken from this page:
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/12026668/comparing-speed-of-haskell-and-c-for-the-computation-of-primes
